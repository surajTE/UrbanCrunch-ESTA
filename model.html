ESTA BUSINESS MODEL

SYSTEMIC PARTNERED ECOSYSTEM.Emirates Specialized Trading Agencies (ESTA) delivers high quality support across the Defense, Military, Government and National Security value chain. We operate a Source, Enable, Execute business model to create broad and wide-ranging benefit to our customers.

SOURCEESTA applies expertise to identifying and delivering the latest advances in technology and mission critical equipment to support our customers in maintaining a strategic edge. We establish partnerships with global leaders to deliver innovation and disruption across the supply chain.

ENABLEESTA enables reach, access and opportunity. We provide agency and distributor enabling capabilities for international companies extending their reach into our markets. We administer and manage commercial ventures into the Defense industry, including partnerships, SPVs, access, networks, registration, local manufacture and influence. We enable business development, create opportunities and manage relationships, inclusive of high-value Defense Offset programs.

EXECUTE.Through our extensive network strength, we deliver high quality services and products. We facilitate local manufacture of military equipment, we stock and supply from our integrated warehouse facilities, and we distribute cutting-edge mission equipment on behalf of world-leading military suppliers. ESTA has the know-how and capability to manage reliable delivery across the span of our supply chain and services.
