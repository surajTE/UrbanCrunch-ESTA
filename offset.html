DEFENSE OFFSET PROGRAM MANAGEMENT

Defense contractors seeking to sell a major military system to the UAE and other Middle East markets are required to offer and fulfill offsets as a condition of sale. ‘Offsets’ have been a feature of Defense procurement contracts for almost forty years and have spread beyond Defense with increasing application into major civil contracts as well.

ESTA is an expert in the UAE Offset program, our team helped to design and introduce it. We will assist you through the entire offset process:

• Design and planning

• Qualification, preparation and facilitation

• Execution and management

We provide many options to apply offsets organically through our Emirates Specialized Group projects, initiatives and investments. Where this is not viable, we do the ground-work and stakeholder management necessary to establish the right offset pathway.

We provide Defense exporters the understanding needed to properly plan for and fulfil offset compliance. Our expert management of offset programs positions our partners to match or exceed their competition.
