ESTA VISION

To be a leading supplier and distributor of products and services into specialist Defense and National Security industries

ESTA MISSION

To pioneer the introduction of cutting-edge equipment and best-in-class international products into the Middle East Defense industry, in order to drive innovation and technological disruption into National Defense and Security capability

ESTA is a pioneering subsidiary of the Emirates Specialized Group, based out of Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates (UAE). We execute and enable trading, distribution, supply chain, local manufacturing and technological services into Defense industries. We maintain extensive international partnerships and vendor networks to enable broad spectrum support as a leading bridge between commercial and military organisations operating into the Middle East, including design and facilitation of Defense Offset programs.

We leverage our position to offer exceptional access to the UAE Defense industry and more broadly into other GCC markets. We apply disruption and innovation to promote continuous improvement and deliver exceptional products and services into the Defense, Military and National Security sectors. We support our customers through the delivery of tomorrow’s Defense and Security solutions today.
